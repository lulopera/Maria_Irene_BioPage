Maria

Maria is an Interactive Media Designer from Colombia. Currently she is living in London, Canada. Maria used to work as anesthesiologist in her home country, but she decided to change paths after she moved to Canada. Maria has dedicated the past years to learn how to develop websites and how to create interactive media contents related to healthcare and Medicine. One of her major goals is to create educational transmedia platforms directed to english and spanish-speaking communities. She has wide experience using different applications and softwares such as Cinema4D, After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop and Premiere.
